Our Story

"Every recipe holds a memory. Every cake carries a little piece of home."

Tempted was born out of a deep love for baking and a belief that homemade always tastes better. Every item is crafted by hand in a home kitchen, using quality ingredients chosen with care. There are no shortcuts here — only slow, thoughtful baking that results in something genuinely worth savouring. Whether it's a birthday cake, a box of cookies, or a loaf of fresh bread, every order gets our full attention and heart.
